Trading Simulation

A trading web app that allows users to trade stocks with virtual money without fear of breaking your wallet!

Technologies used: React, JavaScript, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, and Chart.js, JSON Web Token, RESTful APIs

The following are some of the features of the app.

Overview of account holdings and returns

Research historical data of the stock

Read latest news related to the stock

View order summary after placing a trade

Place a trade with 4 trade types: Buy, Sell, & Buy Cover, & Short Sell

Search for the latest price and intraday price chart of a stock based on its ticker symbol
